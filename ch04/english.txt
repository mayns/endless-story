*Chapter 4.*

In less than five seconds, the silhouette of a malicious homeless was about to pass me by. 
I felt such a joy of being unnoticed that I accidentally happily grunted. 
The bum stopped opposite me and looked me straignt into the eyes. 
And started sadistically slowly to approach, looking at me curiously. 
I can only imagine what he saw, because I personally try to not picture myself at those moments.
— Why did you run away? - he asked simply with a lot of empathy.
— I was saving my life - I said it before realizing what I'm gonna say.
- Why, then, did you stop?
And I answered fast without controlling the mouth again:
— Probably, there was one chance out of two that I still want to die.
— Amusing type. Let's go for a walk.
Then my throat clenched, as I understood everything.
I reluctantly got up from my mossy bed. All that I needed to know, I found out.
